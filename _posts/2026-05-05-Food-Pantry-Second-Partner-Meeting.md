We had been able to hone in on some specific issues as we began devloping the website more in depth such as: what kind of users they would want, who should have access to what, what specific information was most important, etc. I helped to come up with some of these questions by consistently testing our website as if I were one of the intended users. I played a large role in the organization of the home page, nav-bar, and inter-connection of various pages within the website. The idea was to make the end goal reachable in as few clicks as possible and we were moving in a good direction for this to happen. Going forward, I planned to assist in linking the database to the spanish application and making sure that the ease of access was maintained throughout.
