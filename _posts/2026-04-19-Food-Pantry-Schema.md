This post serves as the first in a series of blog posts I will make about the food pantry project a team of peers and I are working on. We are making a website for Conway's First United Methodist Church, specifically their food pantry. The team there asked for us to optimimize and digitize the system they already had in place for intaking and serving community members. Our first meeting with them was very helpful and strongly informed the direction we were taking the project. Following that first meeting, I created a pretty broad entity relationship diagram to represent a general idea for the flow of the website. Aside from that, I have organized a meeting outsite of class with my team and prepard an agenda. As the team manager, I have been doing my best to maintain communication amongst our members. Moving forward, I plan to assist in all areas creating the website to act like a glue to put each piece together nicely.
